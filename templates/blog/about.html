About CodeCanvas

Hi, I'm AK — a software engineer who enjoys building tools, sharing knowledge, and creating platforms that empower others to express themselves.

CodeCanvas isn’t just a blog — it's a canvas for ideas. Whether you’re writing about technology, travel, personal experiences, tutorials, or event recaps, this platform is made for you.

Our Mission

To provide a clean, accessible, and creative space for anyone to publish stories, tutorials, announcements, or reflections — no matter the topic.

What Can You Publish?

  • 📰 Event highlights and announcements
  • 🛠️ Technical guides and how-tos
  • 📸 Personal stories and reflections
  • 🎤 Interviews, opinion pieces, and more

CodeCanvas is designed to be flexible and inclusive — your content, your style.

Behind the Scenes

CodeCanvas is built with Django and React, styled with TailwindCSS, and designed to be fast, clean, and mobile-friendly.
